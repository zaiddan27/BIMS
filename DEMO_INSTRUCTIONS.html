Quick Start

This demo shows how SK users receive notifications when the Barangay Captain approves, rejects, or requests revisions to their project submissions.

Option 1 View Pre-loaded Demo Notifications

The SK dashboard already has 3 demo notifications pre-loaded showing different decision types.

  1. Open dashb.html (SK Dashboard)
  2. Look at the notification bell icon - you'll see a red badge with "3"
  3. Click the notification bell
  4. Click on any notification to see the Captain's comments

Option 2 Test Live Captain Decision Flow

Simulate a real captain decision and see the notification appear in real-time.

  1. Open captain-dashboard.html in a new browser tab
  2. Click "Review" on any pending project
  3. Add comments in the "Comments/Notes" field
  4. Click "Approve Project", "Reject Project", or "Request Revision"
  5. Switch to dashb.html (SK Dashboard) tab
  6. Click the notification bell - you'll see the new notification!
